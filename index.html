Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  3. Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around branches

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned foe their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts about Panda

  1. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day
  2. An adult Panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day
  3. They have great camouflage for their environment

Interesting Facts about Teddy

  1. The most famous Teddy Bears are the Steiff Teddy Bears from Germany
  2. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906
  3. The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’

Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
  3. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  1. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs
  2. Most cicadas have red-orange eyes
  3. Cicadas are true bugs (from the order Hemiptera)
